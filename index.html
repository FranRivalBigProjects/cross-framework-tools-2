❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is resumability vs hydration?

Hydration reactivates a server-rendered app on the client, while resumability allows the app to continue without re-executing all components.

Why does memory usage matter in frameworks?

Lower memory usage improves performance, especially on mobile devices and large-scale applications.

Are newer frameworks faster?

Some modern frameworks optimize performance with new architectures, but maturity and ecosystem also play important roles.

Which rendering strategy is best?

It depends on your use case. SSR, SSG, and islands architecture each have advantages.
